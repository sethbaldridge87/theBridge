About Me





Hi! My name is Bridget and I’m the business owner and primary character performer here at Bridget’s Party Co! I’m so excited to bring an extra world of magic to your child’s party by inviting a princess, hero, or other exciting character to your child’s party! I have experience in theater and performing, and even had the chance to work at EPCOT in Walt Disney World Resort (in Lake Buena Vista, FL) through the Disney College Program. I’m a costumer who creates most of my costumes. I also volunteer with Heroic, Inc., a 501(c)3 charity organization that provides characters for local charity and community events--free of charge. Not only do I have years of experience as a character performer, but I am also experienced with entertaining children as a grade school teacher!

I can’t wait to make your child’s dreams come true with the highest quality character experience! If I don’t have the character you’re looking for, don’t hesitate to contact me because I guarantee I can find it for you!
