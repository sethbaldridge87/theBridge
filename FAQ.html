Frequently Asked Questions





Which areas do you service?

Bridget’s Party Co. is based in North Salt Lake and primarily services Salt Lake and Davis Counties. We are willing to travel so that you can receive the best service possible! Please be aware that locations more than 15 miles outside of North Salt Lake are subject to a $0.50/mile travel fee.

What sets you apart from the competition?

At Bridget’s Party Co., we pride ourself on quality! We feature only high quality costumes, wigs, and makeup that will make you and your child feel like you’ve stepped into a fantasy world! Aside from the best craftsmanship and character appearance, your child will be treated to an immersive experience. Because the character who visits your child is also the owner of this company, you can expect the absolute best.

Are the characters and costumes shown on your website going to be the ones at my party?

Yes. Bridget’s Party Co. only shows pictures on our website of our actual costumes and performers who will make appearances at events.

Are your parties appropriate for all ages?

Yes. Our parties are generally geared for ages 3-10, but all ages are sure to enjoy our authentic and engaging performances. We provide plenty of activities to entertain both girls and boys.

Where do you recommend I hold my party?

Due to the expensive nature and high quality of our costumes and wigs, our preference is to hold parties at locations such as: a home environment, community centers, private rooms at restaurants, etc.

While backyards are popular settings, we ask that in the event of rain, snow, extreme heat, or wind, our performers be provided with an indoor setting. In addition, we have found small parties in the home to be the most successful.

How far in advance should I book?

Our parties book quickly and we recommend booking 4-6 weeks in advance. Parties are booked first come first serve and a completed contract and deposit are required to reserve your date and character.

Do I have to provide anything for the performer?

Our Entertainer will bring everything that they need for their performance! The only thing that we ask you provide is an open area for singing time & games, and a chair for the performer to sit down during story time.

Do I need to supervise the event?

Yes. Bridget’s Party Co. characters are entertainers, and although they are skilled at interacting with children, they are not responsible or liable for the behavior of the children or adults present.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that sometimes things happen and parties need to be canceled or rescheduled. We are happy to help you reschedule your party within 90 days of the original party date (subject to character availability). If you would like to completely cancel your character appearance, you will forfeit your deposit as it is nonrefundable.

Should I tip the performer?

Gratuity is never mandatory. If you loved the performer and thought they were especially magical, then you are welcome to tip.

What if I want a character or activity you do not currently offer?

Don't see what you're looking for? We are more than happy to work with you to build a party that caters to your exact needs. If you’re looking for a character that we don’t offer, don’t hesitate to ask! I have a large network of friends within the Utah Cosplay community and can likely find exactly what you’re looking for!

Do you work with special events, charities, and with sick children?

Yes! Charity cosplay and events is one of my biggest passions. I am also willing to do individual visits as time allows. Please email me, as these events are settled on a case-by-case basis.
